THE WEB COMPETITION

High School Website Competition

For Academic Year 2018 – 2019, RACC proposes as a pilot for a High School Build a Better Website Contest by partnering with Reading Muhlenberg Career & Technology Center and Berks County Career and Technology Center. As a voluntary competition, CTC students will work in teams to create websites for a non-profit organization over the course of six months, September 2018 – January 31, 2018. Websites will be judged in February and RACC will host all the competitors and teachers at its campus.
